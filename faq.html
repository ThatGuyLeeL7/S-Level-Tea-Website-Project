Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Boba Tea?

A: Boba Tea, also known as bubble tea, is a delightful beverage originating from Taiwan. It typically consists of tea, milk, sweetener, and chewy tapioca pearls (boba) that add a unique texture and flavor.

Q: What flavors do you offer?

A: At S Level Tea, we offer a diverse range of flavors, including classic options like milk tea, green tea, and fruit-infused teas. Our menu also features exciting specialty flavors like taro, matcha, and lychee.

Q: Are your drinks customizable?

A: Absolutely! You can customize your Boba Tea to suit your taste preferences. Choose your tea base, sweetness level, and ice preference. Additionally, you can add extra toppings or adjust the boba pearl quantity.

Q: What are the different tea bases you use?

A: We use high-quality black tea, green tea, and oolong tea as our primary bases. Our fruit teas are made with real fruit infusions for a refreshing and natural taste.

Q: Can I adjust the sweetness level in my drink?

A: Yes, you can! Our sweetness levels range from 0% to 100%, allowing you to tailor your drink to your desired sweetness.

Q: Are your drinks lactose-free?

A: We offer lactose-free options! You can choose non-dairy alternatives like almond milk, soy milk, or coconut milk for a delicious and dairy-free experience.

Q: What are the boba pearls made of?

A: Our boba pearls are made from tapioca starch, creating a chewy and delightful texture. They are gluten-free and suitable for vegetarians.

Q: Do you offer vegan options?

A: Certainly! We provide vegan-friendly choices by offering plant-based milk options and ensuring our fruit teas are entirely free from animal products.

Q: How can I place an order?

A: You can place your order online through our website or visit our physical store. We also offer delivery services through selected platforms for your convenience.

Q: Are there loyalty programs or discounts available?

A: Yes, we value our customers! Check our website or follow us on social media to stay updated on promotions, loyalty programs, and special discounts.

Q: How long do the boba pearls last?

A: Our boba pearls are freshly prepared each day to ensure quality. For the best experience, we recommend consuming your Boba Tea within 4-6 hours.
